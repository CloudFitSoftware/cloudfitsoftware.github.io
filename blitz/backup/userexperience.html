Baseline Configuration

Proper tenant setup is critical to ensuring a secure, compliant, and optimized Microsoft 365 environment. Our configuration services include:

Microsoft Cloud Environment Provisioning

Establish a secure Microsoft 365 cloud environment aligned with compliance standards such as NIST 800-171, CMMC V2 L2, and other relevant frameworks, ensuring full readiness for your critical workloads.

Identity and Access Management Configuration

We configure Entra ID, Conditional Access, multi-factor authentication (MFA), Entra ID Protection, and Defender for Cloud Apps to enhance security and minimize risks related to identity and access management across all environments.

Mobile Device Management Setup

Implement Microsoft Intune and Defender for Endpoint for mobile device management (MDM), including enrollment profiles, compliance policies, and security baselines tailored to both mobile devices and Windows endpoints.

Collaboration and Communication Security

Secure collaboration and communication across Microsoft 365 services like Exchange Online, SharePoint Online, OneDrive, and Teams to ensure seamless, secure interaction for both internal and external stakeholders.

Data Governance Implementation

Set up Microsoft Purview for data retention, data labeling, and data loss prevention (DLP), ensuring your organization meets necessary data governance and compliance requirements.

Onboarding Services

We offer a full range of onboarding services to guide your organization through the planning, setup, and hypercare phases of your Microsoft 365 journey, whether you are deploying a new environment or migrating from another platform:

Planning and Strategy

Our team works with you to plan your Microsoft 365 deployment or migration, ensuring a tailored approach that addresses your unique organizational needs. We focus on aligning your business objectives with the technical setup, establishing a strong foundation for your environment.

Migration Scenarios

We specialize in guiding organizations through various migration scenarios to Microsoft 365. Our team ensures smooth transitions for each system type, maintaining security, compliance, and performance throughout the process.

Identity Migration

Migrate from legacy Active Directory or ADFS to Azure AD and Entra ID, manage tenant-to-tenant migrations (including cross-sovereign clouds), and transition from third-party identity providers (e.g., Okta, Ping) to ensure proper synchronization, governance, and compatibility with Microsoft 365 services and security standards.

Data Migration

We support a variety of migration scenarios, including on-premises to cloud, tenant-to-tenant migrations, cross-sovereign cloud moves, and migrations from third-party services like Google Workspace. Our team ensures minimal disruption and a smooth transition to your new M365 environment.

Endpoint Migration

Migrate from legacy System Center Configuration Manager (SCCM) or third-party solutions (e.g., VMware Workspace ONE) to Microsoft Intune and Defender for Endpoint, implementing device enrollment, compliance policies, security baselines, and mobile device management (MDM) to ensure robust endpoint management and enhanced security across platforms.

Hypercare Support

After the initial deployment, we provide hypercare support to ensure a smooth transition to full production. Our team is available to address any challenges, answer questions, and fine-tune the environment as needed during the post-launch period.
